Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 Apr 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Gmail to Telegram Notifier service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Gmail account access (read-only)
  • Telegram chat ID
  • Email metadata (sender, subject, snippet)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send email notifications to your Telegram account
  • Maintain your account connection
  • Improve our service

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Cloudflare Workers KV storage. We store:

  • Gmail access tokens
  • Telegram chat IDs
  • Last processed email IDs

5. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted storage
  • Secure API endpoints
  • Regular security audits

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Modify your notification preferences

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through Telegram.
